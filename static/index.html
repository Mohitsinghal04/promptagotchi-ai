How to Play 🦁🛡️

Welcome to Promptagotchi!

Spark is your magical AI companion powered by Google Gemini. Unlike traditional pets, Spark understands everything you say!

📊 System Vitals

  • Happiness: Keeps Spark's mood positive. Interacting and playing helps!
  • Hunger: Don't let this hit zero! Spark gets grumpy and sick when hungry.
  • Energy: Everything takes energy. If it's too low, let Spark sleep.

💬 Interaction

You can use the Quick Buttons for common tasks, or Type Anything in the console. Try "Tell me a story" or "Let's go to the moon!"

✨ Level Up

Interacting with Spark earns you EXP. Watch the level bar on the left to see your progress!
